OUR REMODELING PROCESS

  • Planning and 3D RenderingStep 1
  • Pulling Up PermitsStep 2
  • DemolitionStep 3
  • Construction WorkStep 4
  • Completion (Final Inspection)Step 5

  • Step 1 : Planning and 3D Rendering

    At Green Renovation Group, everything we do, from designing the floor plan and drafting the 3D design, to performing the construction work, will be to your satisfaction. Our professional crew will provide you with all the information you need to make educated decisions and see your dream home come to life. A laid-out plan will help save you time and money, which is the first step of our five steps of work.

  • Step 2 : Pulling Up Permits

    Our years of experience means we know exactly the right permits to obtain and how to do it quickly. Instead of basing most of the waiting for your project on permits, you can trust that Green Renovation Group will do it expediently so we can start the transformation of your home.

  • Step 3 : Demolition

    At Green Renovation Group, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. At the second step, we will begin demolition discussed areas after obtaining all the proper permits and consulting our experts. In the deepest respect to our clients, we minimize any disruption and destruction that can be avoided.

  • Step 4 : Construction Work

    Choosing the right cabinets, tiles, flooring, paint, etc. can be a tedious job. At Green Renovation Group, we are here to offer you the solutions based on your wishes. We will start with deciding all the right materials together. We will also work on your electrical, water, and other aspects of your home that the project necessitates. At Green Renovation Group we follow city codes. We offer energy-saver fixtures and appliances that will not only help the environment, but also help you save on electrical bills.A

  • Step 5 : Completion (Final Inspection)

    The final step before you are able to enjoy the completed project is a final inspection. This phase is done by a team of professional city inspectors to ensure your home is safe and that the job completed will last for many years to come.
